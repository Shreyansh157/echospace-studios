Recording & Tracking

Our recording service is built around our flagship Vocal Booth (Studio A). We provide an acoustically isolated environment, a selection of high-end microphones (including the Neumann U87), and top-tier preamps (like the Avalon 737) to capture a pristine, radio-ready signal from the start.

Ideal for: Vocals, Solo Instruments, Voice-Overs, and Podcasts.

Book a Recording Session
Mixing & Mastering

This is where your track comes to life. Our engineers will take your raw recordings and balance them, add creative effects, and create a dynamic, punchy, and emotional mix. We work out of our Producer's Desk (Studio C), which features pristine Adam Audio monitors for surgical precision.

After the mix, we offer a separate mastering service to ensure your song is loud, clear, and competitive on all streaming platforms.

Get a Mixing Quote
Band Rehearsal

Our Live Room (Studio B) is a 400 sq. ft., fully soundproofed space designed for full bands. Stop worrying about noise complaints and focus on your performance. The room comes with a full backline (drum kit, bass amp, guitar amps) and a powerful PA system with monitors.

This space is bookable by the hour directly on our booking page.
