The Vocal Booth (Recording Room)

Our premier vocal and instrument booth. This room is acoustically treated for zero reflections, ensuring a pristine, dry recording. It features a Neumann U87 microphone, an Avalon 737 preamp, and industry-standard Pro Tools Ultimate. It's the perfect space for capturing radio-ready sound for lead vocals, voice-overs, or solo instruments.

Address

123 Neon Ave, Room A-101

The Live Room (Rehearsal Room)

This is where you get loud. Our spacious 400 sq. ft. Live Room is fully soundproofed and equipped for a 5-piece band. It includes a Pearl drum kit, Marshall and Ampeg amps, a full PA system, and stage monitors. The variable acoustic panels allow you to tune the room's sound from dead to lively.

The Producer's Desk (Production Room)

The 'in-the-box' creator's dream. This suite is built for inspiration, featuring an ultrawide monitor, Ableton Push 2, a Moog Subsequent 37, and pristine Adam Audio monitors with a subwoofer. It's the perfect, isolated environment to write, produce, mix, and master your next hit without distractions.

    About EchoSpace

    EchoSpace Studios was born from a simple idea: that creativity flourishes in the right environment. We're not just a rental company; we're a community. We've built the spaces we always wanted to work in—rooms that inspire, equipped with gear that just works.
